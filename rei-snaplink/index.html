REI Snaplink

Web Design, UI/UX

REI needed a site refresh for their employee facing intranet and portal. Their previous site was outdated, sluggish, and difficult to navigate. Our strategy for this project was to unify the design around one main focus of search findability in order to build trust in the site as a helpful resource for all REI employees. I assisted with research, prototyping, and designing the UI for this project.
